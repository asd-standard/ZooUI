pyzui.objects.mediaobjects.mediaobjectsutils.svg.svgcache.svgcache module¶

SVG cache management with hashed storage.

Manages hashed SVG cache with svg_ prefix and flat directory structure.

Hash format: svg_{8_char_sha1} Directory: /tmp/pyzui_svg_/ (flat, no subdirectories)
